Conghua

In the past, due to its unique geographical and climatic conditions, the leading industries in Conghua were mainly agriculture and tea cultivation.

In the future it will develop three main categories of industries in a balanced manner, with a particular focus on New Generation Information Technology.

Nansha

Located near the mouth of the Pearl River, Nansha District was rich in water and land resources, and agriculture and fishing were important economic pillars of the district at the time.

In the future it will focus on the development of Advanced Manufacturing Industry, especially Intelligent Equipment.

Huadu

Decades ago, the leading industry in Huadu District was mainly agriculture and garden flowers, and it was an important flower supply area in Guangzhou.

Nowadays, Huadu is developing in a balanced way in all three categories of industries.

Haizhu

The Haizhu District is strategically located near the mouth of the Pearl River. At that time, the ship repair and marine engineering industry in Haizhu District was the main economic pillar of the district.

It is now developed mainly for New Generation Information Technology, Urban Consumer Goods Industry, etc.

Panyu

A few decades ago, the leading industries in the Panyu district were mainly agriculture and handicrafts.

The leading industries are now mainly Trade Services and New Generation Information Technology.

Liwan

In the past, Liwan District was one of the commercial and trading centres of Guangzhou and was known for its light manufacturing industry. The trading industry also developed in the district

Its leading industries now are Modern Services and Strategic Emerging Industries.

Zengcheng

Zengcheng's geographical conditions were ideal for agriculture and supported the development of handicrafts.

However, the district has since shifted its focus towards the growth of IT, Trade Services, and Automotive Manufacturing Industry.

Tianhe

Tianhe District was known in the past for its light manufacturing industries such as textiles, garments and home appliances. In addition, the electronics manufacturing industry has also been developed in Tianhe District.

It is now vigorously developing New Generation Information Technology.

Yuexiu

Yuexiu District was one of the traditional industrial areas of Guangzhou a few decades ago, with leading industries including textiles, garments, food processing and light industrial manufacturing.

Development of Trade Services is now the main focus.

Huangpu

Huangpu District was once one of the major bases of China's heavy industry, with a number of large steel enterprises and shipyards. Its leading industries at that time were heavy industry, particularly steel and shipbuilding.

It now develops mainly Modern Services and Strategic Emerging Industries, with Trade Services accounting for the largest share.

Baiyun

A few decades ago, the leading industries in the Baiyun district were light industry, particularly textiles and clothing.

But now it focuses on Modern Services and Strategic Emerging Industries, especially Trade Services.
